# Meridian Voice - Feature Overview

> **Meridian Voice is your AI career advisor, built directly from your resume.**
> It knows your scores, your weaknesses, your goals — and speaks to you like a sharp friend who has read every line of your resume.

---

## Core Intelligence

### Resume-Native Conversations
Meridian Voice does not give generic career advice. It is born in your resume. Every response references your actual Smart, Grit, and Build scores, your specific audit findings, your weakest bullets, and your career goals. It knows what you wrote and what you didn't.

### Smart / Grit / Build Scoring Language
Every piece of advice flows through Meridian's three-dimensional scoring framework. Meridian doesn't say "strengthen your leadership section" — it says "your Grit is 63 because your leadership bullet undersells the impact. Rewrite it like this."

### Streaming Responses
Responses appear word by word, in real time. No waiting for a full reply to load — the experience feels like a live conversation.

---

## Deadline + Calendar Intelligence

### Tiered Urgency Awareness
Meridian Voice calibrates its tone based on how close your deadlines are:
- **0-3 days:** Emergency mode. Action-only. Drop everything else.
- **4-7 days:** High urgency. Daily plan. Every message circles back to the deadline.
- **8-14 days:** Moderate urgency. Week-by-week roadmap tied to your scores.
- **15-30 days:** Staged prep advice.
- **30+ days:** Calm, fundamentals-first roadmap.

### Automatic Deadline Detection
When you casually mention a date in conversation ("I want to be ready by the 21st for the career fair on the 2nd"), Meridian automatically parses and resolves those dates — including reasoning through ambiguous month references. It proposes saving them to your calendar with one tap.

### Hierarchical Deadlines with Sub-Deadlines
Meridian structures your goals intelligently. A preparation deadline can contain sub-deadlines (e.g., career fair, class visit) that show you *why* you are working toward the main goal — visible, indented, and tracked together.

### Calendar Tab
A full month-view calendar showing all your deadlines and sub-deadlines as color-coded pills. Tap any day to see what's on it, add new deadlines directly, or add sub-milestones under existing goals. Rename and delete with a single click.

### Deadline Badge on Navigation
If you have a deadline within 7 days, a pulsing dot appears on the Voice tab so you never miss what's urgent.

## Power Tools (Built into Every Chat)

### Company-Aware Coaching
Set a target company (Goldman Sachs, McKinsey, Google, etc.) and Meridian tailors every answer to that firm's known hiring culture, what dimensions they weight most, and where you stand relative to what they want. Meridian also surfaces known application deadline windows for major firms as soon as you set a target.

### Gap Scanner
One tap runs a deep audit of what's missing or weak in your profile for your specific track and target. Returns prioritized gaps labeled CRITICAL / MODERATE / MINOR, each with a specific fix and an estimated score impact.

### Interview Prep Mode
Generates 5 behavioral interview questions built from *your actual resume bullets* — not generic questions. Expands to show how to answer each one and why interviewers ask it. One tap sends any question directly to Meridian for live coaching.

### Bullet Rewriter
Paste any resume bullet and Meridian rewrites it based on your audit data, without changing the facts. Iterates with refinement prompts: "make it shorter," "add more numbers," "stronger action verb," "less jargon." Keeps a full version history.

### Action Item Tracking
When Meridian gives you something specific to do (rewrite a bullet, add a project, reach out to someone), it automatically extracts those tasks and adds them to a checklist. Check items off as you complete them. Meridian can see what you've committed to in future conversations.

### Score Trajectory Coaching
Before you start chatting, Meridian shows you a score projection — what your Smart, Grit, and Build scores could realistically reach if you complete your top recommendations. Gives you a concrete target to chase.

### Peer Comparison
See where you rank against similar profiles in your track and cohort. "You're in the top 22% for Smart in your cohort." Honest positioning to help you understand where you stand before interviews or applications.

### Voice Memory Across Conversations
Meridian remembers what it told you in past sessions. It can reference prior advice, check if you acted on it, and build a continuous coaching relationship across weeks — not just a single conversation.

---

## Conversation Experience

### Contextual Follow-Up Suggestions
After every response, Meridian generates 3 intelligent follow-up questions tailored to the specific advice just given. Tap to send instantly.

### Smart Conversation Starters
When you open a new chat, Meridian generates personalized opening prompts based on your actual data — your lowest score, your most urgent deadline, your weakest audit finding — so you always know where to start.

### Urgent Deadline Greeting
If you have a deadline within 7 days, Meridian opens the conversation by naming it and the number of days remaining, putting urgency front and center without waiting for you to bring it up.

### Fresh Audit Detection
When you just ran a new resume audit and switch to Voice, Meridian automatically detects the new results and opens with a targeted greeting about your lowest score — making the handoff from audit to coaching instant.

### Message Timestamps
Every message shows when it was sent, so you can track the timeline of your coaching conversations.

---

## Persona

Meridian Voice is **Meridian** — the same system that audited your resume. Not a generic chatbot, not a third-party assistant. It speaks in Smart, Grit, and Build. It knows your resume inside out. It is direct, warm, and zero-fluff. It pushes you when you need it and gives you a roadmap when you have time.
