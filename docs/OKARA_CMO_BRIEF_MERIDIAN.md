# Meridian — CMO Brief for Okara (UTampa @ $9.99/mo)

**Use this to answer Okara’s question: “What does your USD 9.99/month service actually do for students?”**

---

## The pain point (sharp)

**College students apply to internships and jobs and get ghosted.** They don’t know why. Recruiters spend ~6 seconds on a resume; career centers are overloaded and generic. Students have no “last check” before they hit submit—so they waste applications on resumes that never make it past the first filter.

---

## What the $9.99/mo service actually does

**Meridian is the last check before you apply.** Students verify with a .edu email, upload their resume, and get:

1. **Resume scored like a senior hiring manager** — Smart (academics), Grit (leadership/impact), Build (proof: projects, experience). Track-specific (Pre-Health, Tech, Finance, Consulting, Pre-Law, etc.), so a Pre-Health student is judged on shadowing and clinical exposure; a Tech student on deployed projects and stack.

2. **Concrete, copy-paste edits** — Not “improve your resume.” Specific before/after bullets and line-level fixes: e.g. “Change ‘assisted with patient care’ → ‘Supported 200+ patient visits/week—vitals, intake, EHR—reducing nurse admin load ~15%.’” Or: “Add where your project is deployed; add ‘used by leasing team’ so recruiters see real-world impact.”

3. **Meridian Voice** — A career coach in their pocket that knows their resume. They can ask: “What’s the one thing I should do this week?” “Am I ready for Goldman?” “Run a gap scan for Tech.” “Prep me for the PA program info session.” Answers are grounded in their actual resume and track.

4. **ATS Readiness** — Simulated scan across 4 ATS vendors (Workday, Greenhouse, iCIMS, Lever). They see what gets parsed, what breaks, and “Fix it” rewrites + keyword injection so they pass applicant tracking systems.

5. **Jobs for you** — Roles matched to their profile with match % and “why you fit.” Optional: Apply on Meridian so recruiters get a [Meridian Verified] application with profile/report link.

6. **Unlimited re-audits** — They can edit their resume, re-upload, and see how scores change. First audit is free; then $9.99/mo.

**Price:** $9.99/mo (launch pricing; first audit free). .edu only. We don’t sell their data.
