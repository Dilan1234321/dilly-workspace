Workflow

How a session typically runs

  1. Open a section (experience, projects, leadership). Dilly highlights the lowest-scoring bullets first.
  2. Apply a suggested rewrite or type your own. Scores refresh as soon as you pause.
  3. Compare overall dimension movement in the score strip. If Build barely budged, you know you need proof, not polish.
  4. Ship to PDF or run the ATS scanner to confirm parsers still read you cleanly.

Why live scoring changes behavior

Static templates make you guess. Live scoring closes the loop: you feel which edits matter. That feedback density is how students build intuition fast without a human reviewer on call.

The same editor feeds your coach and your job matches. You are not maintaining three different versions of “what good looks like.”
