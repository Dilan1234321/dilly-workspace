Rhythm

How students actually use it

  1. Save interesting roles during browse sessions. Tag urgency if you are time-boxing effort.
  2. Promote to Applied when you submit. Add submission date for your own sanity.
  3. Log interviews: format, panel, homework. Link prep docs in notes if you want.
  4. Close wins and losses cleanly. Archive rejections so open loops do not clutter the board.

Why pipeline visibility changes strategy

When everything is “I applied somewhere,” you cannot allocate prep. Seeing five roles stuck in Applied with no movement tells you to fix materials before you add ten more.

Tracker plus score plus listings is the triangle: know where you aimed, what you sent, and how strong it was.
