Tailor resume on every job card

Each listing includes a Tailor resume action. Dilly already learned your real experience when you uploaded or built your resume—internships, projects, courses, leadership—and uses that truth to generate a version of your resume aimed at that role’s signals.

No blank ChatGPT prompt. No inventing jobs you did not do. You get the strongest honest framing of what you actually did, aligned to the posting you are looking at—then open it in the live editor to tweak bullets or run ATS.

  • Grounded in your profile: history and bullets Dilly already knows

Job not in Dilly yet? Add it yourself

If a role is not in our listings, enter company, job title, and job description. Dilly builds a tailored resume for that posting the same way: from your real experience, not filler. Use it for niche roles, referrals, or jobs you found on LinkedIn or a company careers page.

Journey

Typical loop

  1. Filter by track, location, or employer type. Start where you are willing to invest real prep time.
  2. Open a listing. Read fit label plus dimension bars. Tap Tailor resume for a draft aligned to that JD, or note the largest gap first.
  3. Act on suggested fixes: refine the tailored draft in the editor, ship a small project for Build, or start a coach thread for strategy.
  4. Apply when the label moves to your risk tolerance. Track the stage in the pipeline automatically.

Why dimension bars beat a percentage match

Percentages feel precise but hide tradeoffs. You might be “82%” with a hollow Build score that still loses to a candidate with shipped work. Dilly keeps the dimensions visible so you invest where recruiters actually compare.

The same scores power your resume audit, so you are not learning a new rubric every screen.
