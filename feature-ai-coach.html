Rhythm

A week with the coach

  1. Monday: strategy pass on top three saved roles. Decide which gap is worth a two-week sprint.
  2. Midweek: short practice bursts on behavioral prompts tied to weak Grit signals.
  3. Post-edit: rerun a mock after resume changes land. Confirm stories match the new bullets.
  4. Friday: lightweight review of tracker + calendar; adjust next week’s plan with coach notes.

Why full context changes the advice quality

Chatbots without your file guess. Dilly coach is constrained by structured signals: scores, stages, deadlines. That constraint is what makes guidance feel like a human who did the homework.
