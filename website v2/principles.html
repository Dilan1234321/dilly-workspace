Principles borrowed from the Duolingo playbook

Use the pattern, not the trademark. Span’s palette below is inspired by the genre (growth green, sky blue, optimism yellow) — adjust for your final brand.

Color & shape

High-contrast primary for CTAs; warm off-white backgrounds; rounded geometry; minimal sharp corporate corners.

Voice

Short sentences. Confident, not corporate. Lowercase can signal approachability on marketing surfaces; product UI should still meet accessibility contrast and clarity rules.

Motion

Micro-celebrations on success; respect prefers-reduced-motion. Never animate critical reading content in a distracting loop.

Trust layers

Consumer = fun. Schools = standards and dashboards. Stakes = science and security. Same logo; different proof blocks.
