The flywheel in three beats

1 · Delight first Short sessions, visible progress, and personality that earns trust — so kids come back and teens don’t feel patronized.
2 · Structure second Standards, dashboards, and proof for teachers and institutions — the same engine, with roles and permissions.
3 · Stakes when ready When outcomes matter (certs, applications, hiring), the UX stays familiar while the bar goes up — no rebrand whiplash.
