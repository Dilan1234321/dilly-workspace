What you actually get in the app

Mobile-first shell: bottom nav for the big moves, deep pages for ATS, applications, certifications, and your full score story. Below is a student-friendly map of the main tabs.

Career Center — command center

  • Compact Smart · Grit · Build row; tap through to your full report and My Score.
  • Ask Meridian entry + chips for quick questions.
  • ATS readiness hub: score, tabs for what parsers see, checklist, fixes, keywords, vendors.
  • Career tools: Gap analysis, cover letter lines, evidence-based interview prep (opens on Center).
  • Habits: streak, weekly review, rituals, “one thing today” — nudges that fit a real week.
  • Shareable recruiter profile link, achievements / sticker sheet, school line (“Meridian for [your school]”).

Rank — cohort context

  • See how you stack in your track with peer-aware framing when stats are available.
  • Motivation without toxicity: compare to learn, not to spiral.

Dilly AI — coach in chat

  • Mock interviews with structured feedback; bullet practice; pitch and common questions.
  • Context handoffs from ATS, certifications, job checklist — same voice, right prompt.
  • Tone settings: encouraging, direct, casual, professional, coach.

Get Hired — pipeline + jobs

  • Applications tracker: Saved → Applied → Interviewing → Offer → Rejected.
  • Jobs with readiness-ranked matches, filters, and apply flows.
  • Career playbook page: cohort lens + resume-grounded deep dive; talk to Dilly from there.
  • Certifications hub: Build-score context, curated cards, “make it land on your resume” → Voice.
  • Insights (trajectory, Am I Ready?, vs peers) lives in the hiring / progress story.

Explore & everything else

  • Explore: track explorer (11 tracks), outreach templates, campus career-center prompts.
  • Practice hub (also reachable from quick links): mock interview, bullets, pitch, prep modes.
  • Companies we know — verified employer pages with score bars and voice-friendly guidelines.
  • Meridian Profile (full) with privacy toggles for what recruiters can see.

Onboarding (first session)

  1. Welcome → .edu verification code.
  2. Profile: name, majors/minors, application target; track hints for pre-professional paths.
  3. Upload resume → scanning animation → score reveal and gap callout.
  4. Land on Career Center with your numbers ready.
