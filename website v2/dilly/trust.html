Trust is the product.

Delight gets you in the door; integrity keeps the network valuable. Dilly inherits Meridian’s bias toward explainable scores and student-owned data.

  • .edu verification — student-only accounts.
  • Your data is yours; we don’t sell it.
  • HTTPS, clear security posture; download-your-data path in product.
  • Voice transparency: explain why; admit uncertainty when evidence is thin.
  • Optional controls for what persists from chat to profile.
  • Prestige-neutral scoring — evidence and impact over brand-name flex.

Meridian Truth Standard (MTS)

Scores and recommendations should trace back to what you actually wrote. No hollow hype. If it’s wrong, the bug is in the engine — not the student’s dignity.

When on, Voice memories can enrich coaching. When off, chat stays ephemeral beyond what you choose to save.
