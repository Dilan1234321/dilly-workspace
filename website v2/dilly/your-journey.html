One app, four years

Same playful clarity as the Duolingo-style ladder — but Dilly’s output is hire-ready proof: audits, ATS, applications, and coach sessions that escalate as stakes do.

Year 1 · foundation

Learn how you read on paper

First resume pass, first Smart/Grit/Build snapshot, first “what ATS sees” moment. Build the habit of updating after every win.

  • Explore tracks; set a primary goal.
  • Use Dilly AI for bullet practice and club stories.
↓
Year 2 · depth

Turn experience into evidence

Internships and projects land on the resume; rerun audits to see dimensions move. ATS checklist becomes a ritual before every app.

  • Mock interviews with STAR feedback.
  • Start logging applications in the pipeline.
↓
Year 3 · velocity

Volume without chaos

Jobs tab, filters, and readiness ranks cut noise. Playbook and certifications pages help you choose what actually moves Build.

  • Target firms for tailored Voice prompts.
  • Weekly review ritual so nothing slips.
↓
Year 4 · stakes

Offer season with receipts

Shareable profile link in your footer. Full Meridian profile when you want depth. Every score tied to something you wrote — recruiter questions get answers.

  • Am I Ready? against real roles.
  • Outcome capture (interview/offer) for your story — optional, your consent.
