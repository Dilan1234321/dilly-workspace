Where Duolingo ends and Meridian begins

Duolingo optimizes for language reps and daily habit. Your north star is talent truth and career acceleration. The bridge: use their ladder and delight, keep your integrity bar.

From Duolingo, keep…

  • One brand across ages
  • Free core + clear upgrades
  • Teacher/institution overlays
  • Science + playful UX pairing

For Meridian, insist on…

  • Explainable scores (MTS)
  • Prestige-neutral, evidence-first audits
  • No dark patterns on “failure”
  • Privacy-by-design for resumes

Combined path

Student meets Span in middle school with low-stakes skill games → high school adds structured progress and teacher visibility → college layers in resume truth, interview prep, and hiring-ready proof. Same account, escalating seriousness.
