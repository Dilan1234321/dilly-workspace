How does scoring work?

Smart measures academic rigor. Grit measures leadership and quantifiable impact. Build measures proof — deployed projects, internships, clinical hours. Each is 0-100, weighted by your career track.

Is my data safe?

.edu verification only. We never sell your resume or data. Encrypted, stays yours.

What if I don't have much experience?

Dilly helps most when you have less. We show what's missing and how to frame what you already have.

How is Dilly different from ChatGPT?

ChatGPT gives generic advice. Dilly knows your resume, scores, applications, deadlines, and career track.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No contracts. Your data stays even if you downgrade.
