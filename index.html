FAQ
Questions?
How does scoring work?

Dilly scores your resume across three dimensions: Smart (academic rigor, GPA signals, certifications), Grit (leadership, quantified impact, extracurriculars), and Build (track-specific proof — deployed projects, internships, clinical hours). Each dimension is scored out of 100, and your composite Dilly Score tells you exactly where you stand.

Is my data safe?

.edu email required. We never sell your data. Your resume is encrypted and stays yours. No recruiter sees anything without your explicit opt-in.

What if I don't have much experience?

That's exactly when Dilly helps the most. Most students don't know what's missing — Dilly shows you the specific gaps and gives you a concrete plan to close them, even if you're starting from scratch.

How is Dilly different from ChatGPT?

ChatGPT gives generic advice. Dilly knows your actual resume, your scores, your applications, your deadlines, and your target companies. It gives you specific, actionable coaching — not "add more action verbs."

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel through the App Store anytime. No lock-in. Your data stays even if you downgrade to the free tier.
