Process

How a scan runs

  1. Upload the exact file you will submit. Dilly analyzes structure and text layers the way parsers do.
  2. Select target systems or run the full battery. Compare scores when you are shotgun-applying across industries.
  3. Read the extraction preview. If your internship title disappears, fix it before you click apply.
  4. Apply fixes, then rescan. Ship when the weakest engine crosses your comfort threshold.

Why multi-system matters

Students optimize for one checker and still get filtered elsewhere. Dilly surfaces divergence between engines so you are not fooled by a single green badge.

Pair scans with your Dilly Score: content can be strong while structure still blocks you. Fixing both is how you stop “great candidate, never seen.”
