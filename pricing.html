Questions

Is the free tier actually useful?

Yes. Dilly Starter gives you a real score compared to actual students in your cohort, a basic ATS formatting check, and full access to job listings. It's a genuine diagnostic — not a teaser. If you just want to see where you stand, Starter does that for free, forever.

What's the difference between Starter and Dilly?

Starter tells you what's wrong. Dilly fixes it. AI line-by-line rewrites, full ATS scanning across 6 vendors, cohort benchmarking across 22 fields, personalized job matching, and mock interviews. Starter is the diagnosis. Dilly is the treatment.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, anytime. No contracts, no penalties. Cancel through the App Store or your account settings. If you cancel, your Dilly Starter access stays forever. Your score history and profile are never deleted.

What ATS systems does Dilly scan?

Greenhouse, Lever, Workday, iCIMS, Taleo, and Ashby — the six systems used by the overwhelming majority of companies that hire college graduates and interns. We test your resume against all six, not just one.

How accurate is the cohort scoring?

Dilly scores you against real students in your specific field — not a generic rubric. Your score updates as the cohort changes, so you always know where you actually stand relative to the people competing for the same roles.

What if I don't see results?

If you use Dilly for 30 days, go through the AI resume audit, and don't feel it improved your applications — email us. We'll make it right. We'd rather earn your trust than keep $9.99.
