Pricing FAQ

What's a founding member?

Early users get Dilly Pro at $4.99/month — locked in. When we raise the price to $9.99/month, your rate stays the same for as long as you're subscribed. It's our way of thanking early believers.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel through the App Store. No contracts, no penalties. Your data and score history stay even if you downgrade to free.

Is the free tier actually useful?

Yes. You get a full audit with Smart/Grit/Build scores, can browse all internships with readiness badges, and get 3 AI messages per day. It's enough to see where you stand. Pro unlocks the tools to actually fix it.

Do you offer student discounts?

Dilly IS the student discount. $4.99/month is already less than a coffee. We're built for students — the founding member price is the lowest we'll ever go.

What payment methods do you accept?

Subscription is through the App Store. Apple Pay, credit card, debit card — whatever's on your Apple ID.
