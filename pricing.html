FAQ

Common questions

Can I try Dilly before paying?

Yes. The free tier gives you unlimited rule-based resume audits, a basic score, and access to job listings. You can see if Dilly works for you before upgrading.

What's the difference between rule-based and AI-powered audits?

Rule-based audits check for formatting, structure, and keyword presence. AI-powered audits read your resume like a senior hiring manager would, analyzing the quality of your bullet points, the strength of your narrative, and giving you specific rewrites tailored to your target career field.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel from your account settings and you won't be charged again. Your data stays yours.

Is there a student discount?

Dilly is already priced for students. $9.99/mo is cheaper than a DoorDash order. The annual plan at $79/year saves you 34% and comes out to $6.58/mo.

Do you sell my data?

Never. Dilly is .edu verified and student-first. Your resume, scores, and personal information are never sold to third parties.

What's the desktop command center?

Pro members get access to app.hellodilly.com, a full desktop career workspace with your Career Genome, application tracker, calendar, leaderboard, and AI advisor all in one place. Think of it as your personal career center's headquarters.
