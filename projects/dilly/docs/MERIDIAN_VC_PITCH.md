# Meridian AI — Investor Pitch

**The Career Infrastructure for the AI Economy**

---

## The Problem

AI is dismantling the entry-level job market.

Between 2024 and 2030, an estimated 300 million jobs globally will be affected by AI automation (Goldman Sachs, 2023). The roles hit first and hardest are entry-level white-collar positions: junior analysts, associate copywriters, first-year accountants, support engineers, administrative coordinators. These are the exact roles that 4 million US college graduates compete for every year.

The consequences are already visible:

- **Fewer openings.** Companies that hired 5 junior analysts now hire 2 and give them AI tools. McKinsey estimates 30% of hours worked in the US could be automated by 2030.
- **Higher bars.** Entry-level job descriptions now require 2-3 years of experience, AI fluency, and demonstrated output — not just a degree.
- **Broken signals.** AI writes every resume, every cover letter, every LinkedIn summary. When every candidate looks identical on paper, employers can't differentiate. Students spray 200 applications and hear nothing.
- **Career centers can't keep up.** University career centers average 1 advisor per 1,700 students. They're understaffed, under-tooled, and unable to provide the personalized, data-driven guidance students need.

The result: a generation of college graduates locked out of the workforce. Student loan debt is at $1.77 trillion. ROI on a degree is increasingly questioned. And the institutions responsible for career outcomes — universities — have no modern infrastructure to fix it.

This is not a future problem. It is happening now.

---

## The Solution

**Meridian is the career infrastructure platform for college students entering the AI economy.**

We don't write resumes. We don't list jobs. We build the verified talent layer that sits between students, universities, and employers — powered by AI that works *for* students, not against them.

### What Meridian Does

**For students:** A career accelerator that audits their resume against real employer criteria, coaches them through AI-powered voice, matches them to opportunities where they actually fit, and builds a verified profile that proves what they can do — not just what they claim.

**For universities:** A scalable career center platform with cohort analytics, outcome tracking, and employer engagement tools. One Meridian deployment replaces a dozen disconnected systems.

**For employers:** Access to a verified, audited, ranked pool of early-career talent. No more sifting through 500 identical resumes. Meridian surfaces the candidates who actually match, with evidence.

### Core Platform

| Layer | What It Does |
|-------|-------------|
| **Meridian Audit Engine** | AI-powered resume scoring across three dimensions (Smart, Grit, Build) calibrated per career track. 11 tracks: Tech, Finance, Pre-Health, Pre-Law, Consulting, Business, Science, Communications, Education, Arts, Humanities. Prestige-neutral. Evidence-cited. |
| **Meridian Voice** | Conversational AI career coach. Knows the student's resume, scores, goals, target companies. Runs gap analysis, mock interviews, bullet rewrites, interview prep, and emotional support. Available 24/7. |
| **ATS Intelligence** | Simulates how 4 major ATS platforms (Workday, Greenhouse, iCIMS, Lever) parse the same resume. Company-to-ATS lookup for 100+ employers. Auto-rewrites flagged bullets. No other product does this. |
| **Matching Engine** | Semantic search + skill-fit + Meridian score matching. Employers describe what they need; Meridian ranks candidates from the verified pool. Students set preferences; jobs find them. |
| **Verified Profiles** | Six-second recruiter-facing profiles with audited scores, cited evidence, and shareable links. Auto-updated. Designed for the 6-second recruiter scan. |
| **Career Intelligence** | Company hiring criteria database, recruiter advice, AI displacement risk per role, labor market trends. Students make decisions with data, not guesswork. |

## Why Now

Three forces are converging:

1. **AI displacement is accelerating.** Every quarter, another wave of companies restructures entry-level roles around AI. The window for building the solution is now — before the crisis is fully realized and before incumbents react.

2. **Universities are desperate for outcomes infrastructure.** Enrollment is declining. Accreditors are demanding employment data. Parents are questioning tuition ROI. Career centers need a platform, not more headcount. The buyer is ready.

3. **Resumes are dying as a signal.** When AI generates every application document, the resume becomes noise. The market needs a new credentialing layer — verified, audited, evidence-backed. That's Meridian.

## Product — What's Built Today

Meridian is not a slide deck. The platform is live, in production, and actively used by students at the University of Tampa.

### Current Capabilities (Live)

- **Full audit engine** with LLM-powered scoring across Smart (academic rigor), Grit (sustained effort), Build (tangible output). 11 career tracks with track-specific criteria sourced from employer hiring guidelines.
- **ATS Readiness suite** — 4-vendor simulation (Workday, Greenhouse, iCIMS, Lever), keyword density analysis, auto-bullet rewrites, contextual keyword injection, company-to-ATS lookup (100+ companies), section reorder suggestions. No competitor offers this depth.
- **Meridian Voice** — Conversational AI that knows the student's full profile. Runs mock interviews, gap analysis, ready checks, interview prep, cover letter generation. Captures beyond-resume data (skills, experiences, relationships) through natural conversation.
- **Job matching** — Ethical job scraping (Greenhouse API, USAJobs), company hiring criteria database, personalized match scoring, "Apply Through Meridian" (verified application delivery).
- **Recruiter search API** — Semantic + skill-fit + Meridian-score matching. Employer-facing search with JD-to-Meridian-fit conversion.
- **Application tracker** — Full pipeline: Saved → Applied → Interviewing → Offer → Rejected. Stats, notes, deadline integration.
- **Shareable profiles** — Six-second recruiter-facing profiles with auto-updating scores and evidence.
- **Career Hub** — Timeline, search, connections, progress tracking, decision log. A second brain for career planning.
- **Templates** — Cover letters, thank-you notes, follow-ups, LinkedIn messages. All personalized from the student's Meridian profile.
- **Practice modes** — Mock interviews (structured, scored), bullet practice, 60-second pitch, common questions, company-specific prep.
- **Achievements + gamification** — 15 achievements, streaks, daily micro-actions, profile themes, share cards. Habit-forming design.
- **Parent features** — Gift Meridian, family plan, milestone notifications, parent summary dashboard. Monetization through the people who actually pay for college.
- **Company pages** — Verified employer profiles with score bars, hiring criteria, open roles, recruiter advice.

### Tech Stack

- **Backend:** Python/FastAPI, OpenAI (GPT-4o, GPT-4o-mini), embeddings for semantic search
- **Frontend:** Next.js, React, Tailwind, mobile-first (375px viewport)
- **Auth:** .edu-only verification (email code, no passwords)
- **Data:** Structured JSON profiles, file-based storage (designed for migration to PostgreSQL/Supabase at scale)
- **Infrastructure:** Ready for cloud deployment (Vercel + Railway/Fly.io)

## The Vision: 2028 and Beyond

**Meridian becomes the verified talent layer for early-career employment.**

In the fully realized vision:

- A student onboards at freshman orientation. Over 4 years, Meridian tracks their growth: every skill gained, every project completed, every experience verified.
- When they're ready to enter the workforce, they don't write a resume. They have a Meridian profile — audited, scored, verified, with 4 years of evidence.

- The resume is obsolete. The job board is obsolete. The spray-and-pray application process is obsolete. What replaces it is a verified talent marketplace where real skills meet real opportunities.

**This is the Credit Score for Talent.** A number that means something because it's backed by evidence, calibrated against employer criteria, and verified by the platform — not self-reported by the candidate.
