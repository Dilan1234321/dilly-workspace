# Meridian Career Center — A career center open 24/7

**Tagline:** *Meridian: A career center open 24/7.*

This doc lists features that make the Meridian Career Center section deliver that feel: whatever a student would get from walking into a physical career center, they can get in-app, anytime.

---

## Core idea

A physical career center has office hours. Meridian Career Center is **always on**: one dashboard where resume review, advice, resources, and progress live together, with **Meridian Voice** (the chatbot) as the advisor you can talk to at 11 p.m.

---

## Features to deliver the 24/7 career center feel

### 1. **Meridian Voice — Talk to an advisor anytime**

- **What it is:** The chatbot (Meridian Voice) in the Career Center. Student can ask anything: “Should I list my high school job?” “How do I explain a gap?” “What do Tech recruiters care about?”
- **24/7 feel:** No “drop in Tuesday 2–4.” They get an answer in the moment, in the same tone as Meridian Hiring Manager (expert, direct, constructive). Optional: context-aware (knows their last audit, track, major) so answers are tailored.
- **Ideas link:** IDEAS #24 (One “Ask Meridian” per week), #28 (Daily Companion AI). Can start as on-demand Q&A, add proactive nudges later.

### 2. **Resume audit on demand**

- **What it is:** Run a full Meridian audit (Smart, Grit, Build, red flags, recommendations) from the Career Center. Same flow as today; in this context it’s “get your resume reviewed” without waiting for an appointment.
- **24/7 feel:** “I’m up at midnight polishing my resume — I can get feedback now.” Already built; in Career Center it’s the hero action: “Run audit” or “Get resume feedback.”

### 3. **Your Meridian story — Progress over time**

- **What it is:** History of audits, “why your scores changed” (explain-delta), and optional goals or targets (e.g. “Land summer analyst at Goldman”). One place to see improvement and next steps.
- **24/7 feel:** Like an advisor who remembers you. “Last time you were 72 Grit; here’s what moved the needle.” No need to re-explain your story every visit.
- **Ideas link:** Progress + explain-delta already exist; goals/deadlines in IDEAS #16, #18.

### 4. **Quick answers & resources**

- **What it is:** Short, scannable content: resume tips, interview FAQs, “When to add GPA,” “How to format dates,” LinkedIn one-pagers. Search or browse by topic.
- **24/7 feel:** “It’s 10 p.m. and I have a question about my resume format.” They get an answer or a 2‑minute read instead of waiting for workshop week.
- **Ideas link:** Can pull from workshops (#3) or training (#6) later; start with a small curated set.

### 5. **Mock audit / job-fit check**

- **What it is:** User pastes (or selects) a job description; Meridian scores their resume as if that employer’s bar is the standard. “How does my resume stack up for this role?”
- **24/7 feel:** “Career center would do a mock review for this posting; I can do it tonight.” Reduces “am I wasting my time applying?” anxiety.
- **Ideas link:** IDEAS #14 (Unlimited mock audits), #17 (“Am I Ready?” one-tap check).

### 6. **Interview prep from your evidence**

- **What it is:** “Recruiter asks: Tell me about leadership. Here’s how to use your Meridian Grit evidence.” Bullets or 30‑second scripts grounded in their actual resume (Smart/Grit/Build).
- **24/7 feel:** Like mock-interview prep with an advisor, but on demand. They’re not generic tips; they’re “use this bullet, say this.”
- **Ideas link:** IDEAS #23 (Interview prep from evidence).

### 7. **Goals, deadlines & sprint**

- **What it is:** Optional: user sets goals (“Summer analyst at Goldman,” “Get into med school”) and/or deadlines. Career Center shows countdown; near deadline, a short “sprint” (to-dos) so they use time left well.
- **24/7 feel:** Advisor would say “Your deadline is in two weeks — here’s what to do first.” We surface that every time they open the app.
- **Ideas link:** IDEAS #16 (Goal setting & banner), #18 (Deadline countdown + sprint plan).

### 8. **One home — Career Center dashboard**

- **What it is:** Single screen (or tab): Meridian Voice (chat) prominent; “Run audit” / “Last audit” / “Your progress”; quick links to resources, mock audit, interview prep. Optional: upcoming events (career fair, workshops) if we have data.
- **24/7 feel:** “This is my career center.” One place, not scattered features. Open the app → you’re in the center; Voice is the person you talk to.

### 9. **Shareable snapshot & report**

- **What it is:** After an audit, they can share a one-pager (radar + score + one-line summary) or the full PDF report. For Handshake note, LinkedIn DM, career fair follow-up.
- **24/7 feel:** Advisor would say “Send them this.” We give them the asset and the link anytime.
- **Ideas link:** IDEAS #19 (Shareable Meridian Snapshot); PDF report already exists.

### 10. **Cover letter / outreach lines (Meridian-aware)**

- **What it is:** Generate opening lines or cover-letter hooks that cite their real Smart/Grit/Build evidence. Not generic; tailored to their resume.
- **24/7 feel:** “I need to email a recruiter tonight.” One tap from profile to get a line that uses their story.
- **Ideas link:** IDEAS #21 (Meridian-aware cover letter / outreach lines).
