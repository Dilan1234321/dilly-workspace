# Application target — quality rubric

Use this when spot-checking audits to confirm tailoring meets the bar. One run per target should satisfy the checks below.

---

## Internship

- [ ] **At least one recommendation** explicitly references internship (e.g. starts with "For your internship applications," or "Internship recruiters look for").
- [ ] **meridian_take** opens with "For internship applications," or equivalent, then the punchy takeaway.
- [ ] **audit_findings** use internship-appropriate language (learning, growth, short-term contribution, coachability); no "when you join as a full-time" framing.
- [ ] No language that assumes they are applying for senior or long-term full-time roles.

---

## Full-time job

- [ ] **At least one recommendation** explicitly references full-time hiring (e.g. "For full-time roles," or "Full-time hiring managers want to see").
- [ ] **meridian_take** opens with "For full-time roles," or "For full-time hiring," then the punchy takeaway.
- [ ] **audit_findings** use full-time-appropriate language (ownership, impact, readiness, accountability).
- [ ] No framing that assumes they are only seeking internships or "exploring."

---

## Grad school

- [ ] **At least one recommendation** explicitly references admissions or their path (e.g. "For med school applications," or "Admissions committees look for").
- [ ] **meridian_take** opens with "For [med/law/grad] applications," or "For admissions," then the punchy takeaway.
- [ ] **audit_findings** use admissions-appropriate language (research, rigor, fit for program).
- [ ] No framing that assumes they are only applying to jobs.

---

## Exploring

- [ ] **At least one recommendation** frames for both paths (e.g. "whether you're applying to internships or early roles").
- [ ] **meridian_take** references versatility or the one move that helps across the board.
- [ ] **audit_findings** and recommendations are broadly useful; no single-path assumption.

---

*If a run fails a check, tighten the prompt or add negative instructions. See APPLICATION_TARGET_QUALITY_PLAN.md for the full quality plan.*
