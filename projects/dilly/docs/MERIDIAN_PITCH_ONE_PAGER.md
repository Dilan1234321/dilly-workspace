# Meridian — One-pager for founder/advisor conversations

**Use this when meeting with entrepreneurs, advisors, or potential connectors (e.g. Kevin Harrington–type intros).**

---

## What it is (30 seconds)

**Meridian** is a career readiness app for college students. We score their resume the way a senior hiring manager would—on **Smart** (acuity, clarity, fit), **Grit** (drive, leadership, resilience), and **Build** (proof: experience, projects, impact)—and tell them exactly what to fix before they apply. Think of it as "run your last check before Handshake."

We're **student-first and school-specific**: starting with University of Tampa ("Meridian for Spartans"), .edu-only sign-up, and a promise they'll recognize: *"We don't sell your data to recruiters."*

---

## Why it matters

- **Handshake and LinkedIn make students feel like a number.** We treat them like the candidate they actually are: one place for their resume, their scores, and their plan. No more guessing.
- **Recruiters spend seconds on a resume.** Students have one shot. We give them the bar—and the edits—before they hit submit.
- **Career centers are stretched.** We're a career center in their pocket: 24/7 audit, track-specific playbooks (Pre-Health, Pre-Law, Tech, etc.), and a chat (Meridian Voice) for career Q&A.

---

## What's built (today)

- **Onboarding:** .edu verification, school theme (UTampa), name/major/track/goals, "What is Meridian" tailored to them, resume upload, payment ($9.99/mo; dev-unlock for testing).
- **Main app:** Career Center (home), Resume Review (upload → audit → scores, findings, recommendations, PDF, share), Meridian Voice (chat). Bottom nav, profile, peer percentiles ("Top X% in your track"), red flags, 1-page nudge.
- **Backend:** Auth, profile API, audit API (PDF/DOCX → scores + evidence + recommendations), explain-delta, report PDF, Stripe placeholder. Ready for Phase 3 (polish, mobile, launch).

**First campus:** University of Tampa. Horizon: fully functional, launch-ready by April.

---

## Coming to the Career Center (roadmap)

We're adding the kinds of things students get from Handshake, LinkedIn, and tools like Quinncia—but in one place, 24/7, without selling their data:

- **Interview prep** from their actual resume (scripts and talking points from their Meridian evidence).
- **Mock audit for a job** — paste a JD; we score their resume against that role ("Am I ready?").
- **Internships & jobs** by major/track (JobBook-style discovery; we own "get ready," easier than Handshake for finding).
- **Workshops & resources** in-app (interview prep, LinkedIn tips, resume FAQs).
- **Shareable badge** for LinkedIn (e.g. "Meridian 85" / "100 Grit").
- **Goals & deadlines** with countdown and sprint plan.
- **Mentors** — directory or matching to Meridian-affiliated mentors.

So the pitch isn't "one resume tool"—it's *the career center in their pocket*.
