FAQ for parents

Is Dilly safe for my student?

Yes. We use .edu-only sign-up (verified), no password (verification code only), and we don't sell or share their data. Resumes are processed for scoring and never shared with third parties.

Who can see their resume?

Only your student. Parents never see the resume unless the student explicitly emails them a shareable report (PDF). If they invite you to their “parent view,” you only see scores, last audit date, and an “on track” summary—no resume content.

Can I see how they're doing?

Only if your student invites you. They can generate a private link that shows you: their name, track, last audit date, scores (Smart, Grit, Build), and whether they're “on track.” They can also email you their Dilly report (PDF) anytime. You never get access to their account or full resume.
