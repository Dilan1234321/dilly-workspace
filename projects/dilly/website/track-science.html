What we score

Smart

Academic Rigor

Science major (Biology, Chemistry, Physics, etc.), GPA, rigorous coursework, honors thesis. We reward difficulty—a 3.5 in Orgo means more than a 4.0 in Gen Ed.

Grit

Sustained Commitment

Long-term lab commitment, teaching/tutoring, work experience. Owned outcomes and follow-through—not one-off stints. Duration and scope matter.

Build

Research Profile

Lab experience with PI and methods. Publications, posters, preprints. Quantified impact in research bullets. Industry or academic research internships.

Gaps we fix

These kill science applications. We catch them and tell you exactly how to fix them.

No lab experience before senior year Critical Start reaching out to professors now. Cold email with your transcript and interest. Even a semester in a lab adds critical Build signal.
Research bullets lack methods Critical Every bullet needs: technique (PCR, HPLC, etc.), scope (n=50, 6 months), outcome (finding, poster, publication). We give you copy-paste rewrites.
No quantified impact in research Moderate Add numbers: sample size, accuracy, throughput, significance. Even "contributed to 3 publications" helps. We show you exactly how.
