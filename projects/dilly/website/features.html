What's in the app

Resume audit, ATS scan, jobs, Dilly Voice, playbooks, achievements, and your shareable profile.

Resume Audit

Upload your resume. Get Smart, Grit, and Build scores with evidence from your actual content. Copy-paste line edits, red flags, and strategic recommendations—tailored to your track.

Sample finding: "Your Grit score is held back by vague verbs. Change 'assisted with' to 'supported 200+ visits/week'—quantify impact."

Dilly Voice

Your career coach in your pocket. Ask anything—Dilly knows your resume, track, and goals. Run gap scans, "Am I ready?" checks, and bullet rewrites from natural language. Multi-tab chats, tone settings, and "Remember this" notes so advice stays personal.

Example: "You: Am I ready for Goldman? Dilly: Runs a ready check, then: 'Stretch—add one quantified outcome to your consulting club bullet and you're in the green.'"

ATS Readiness

See what recruiters' systems actually parse. One scan: 0–100 score, what each ATS extracts (name, contact, experience, skills), formatting checklist, and per-vendor simulation (Workday, Greenhouse, iCIMS, Lever). Fix It rewrites weak bullets; keyword injection shows exactly where to add missing terms. Company lookup: type "Amazon" and see how your resume scores for their ATS.

Example: "Workday: 72 (pass). Greenhouse: 68 (risky). Your target company uses Workday—here's what breaks and what to fix."

Jobs for you

Dilly-verified jobs run through your resume. Match % and "why you fit" bullets. Set locations (cities or "Open to anywhere"). Bookmark and organize into collections. Apply on Dilly: we send your application so recruiters see [Dilly Verified] in the subject—profile and report link included.

Example: "85% match—Data Science Intern. Why: Your predictive engine project aligns with their ML stack; your Grit evidence (leadership) fits their culture note."

Track-Specific Playbooks

Pre-Health, Pre-Law, Tech, Finance, Consulting, and more. Each track has its own playbook: what recruiters look for, what to add, and how to stand out.

Example: Pre-Health: "List shadowing hours and setting—e.g. '40 hrs shadowing in ED.' Add clinical or volunteer role with patient contact. Include research if you have it; med schools look for both."

Peer Percentiles

See where you stand: "Top 10% Grit in Pre-Health" or "Top 15% Build for Tech." Apples-to-apples comparison with others in your field.

Example: "You're in the top 12% for Grit among Tech students—strong leadership evidence. Build is 45th percentile; add a deployed project to move up."

Progress Over Time

Re-audit after edits. See why your scores changed. Track improvement and know exactly what's working before your next application.

Example: "Build +8 after you added 'Deployed on Vercel' and linked GitHub. Dilly's take: 'Track-specific proof is now visible to recruiters.'"

Shareable Report & Six-Second Profile

Download a professional PDF. Get a shareable link: one page recruiters can scan in 6 seconds—scores, evidence, goal, photo. Updates automatically when you edit your profile or run a new audit. Add the link to your resume footer; "See what recruiters see" preview in the app.

Example: "Full profile: dilly-careers.com/p/abc123. Recruiters see your tagline, scores, key findings, and career goal in one view."

Achievements & Share Cards

Unlock achievements (first audit, Top 25%, triple threat, century club, first application, first interview, and more). Sticker-style collection; pick up to 3 for share cards. Download badge or snapshot with your chosen achievements and custom tagline for LinkedIn or Handshake.

Example: "Top 12% Grit · First audit · Triple threat. Dilly Careers."
