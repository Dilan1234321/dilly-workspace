How it works

Guided onboarding, then a four-tab home built for mobile.

.edu code → Profile → Upload & scan → Scores → Main app
1

Sign up with your .edu email

No password—we email a verification code. Returning users with a session skip straight to home.

2

Build your profile

Name, multiple majors and minors (with school autocomplete where supported), application target, and pre-professional path when it applies—so your cohort and playbook match how you actually recruit.

3

Upload your resume

PDF or DOCX (or paste text from settings later). We parse it, score Smart / Grit / Build, and show evidence-backed fixes. Your first run includes a guided scanning moment before the score reveal.

4

Use the four-tab shell

Career Center for scores, ATS, tools, and habits. Rank for leaderboard. Dilly AI for chat (including mock interview). Get Hired for applications and jobs—plus playbook, certifications, and checklist. Insights, Calendar, Practice, and Explore stay one tap away via quick links.

How we score

Transparent. Evidence-based. No black box.

Dilly Truth Standard

Scores come from your resume only. We never invent facts. If you don't say it, we don't score it.

Track-specific

Pre-Health weighs shadowing and clinical hours. Tech weighs deployed projects and stack. We score like recruiters in your field.

Evidence cited

Every score ties to a line in your resume. We show you exactly what we saw—and what's missing.

Prestige-neutral

We don't favor Ivy League. We reward difficulty, impact, and proof—not school name.
