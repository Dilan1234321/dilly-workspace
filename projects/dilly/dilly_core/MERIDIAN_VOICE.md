# Meridian Voice — Product names

**Dilly** is the name of the **chatbot** in the app (formerly Meridian Voice). It’s the AI you talk to for career advice, questions, and guidance.

**Meridian Career Center** is a **section of the app**: a full career-center experience on your phone. It includes Dilly (the chatbot) integrated into a dashboard so it feels like a powerful career center in your pocket. **Tagline:** *Meridian: A career center open 24/7.* Feature set: `projects/meridian/docs/MERIDIAN_CAREER_CENTER_FEATURES.md`.

**Meridian Hiring Manager** is the **audit persona**: the unified voice (hiring manager + job consultant + career advisor) used for resume feedback, red flags, and recommendations. See `MERIDIAN_HIRING_MANAGER.md` for tone and principles.

---

| Name | What it is |
|------|------------|
| **Meridian Hiring Manager** | Audit persona — resume feedback, red flags, recommendations (see MERIDIAN_HIRING_MANAGER.md) |
| **Dilly** | The in-app chatbot (formerly Meridian Voice) |
| **Meridian Career Center** | App section — career center dashboard with Dilly integrated |
