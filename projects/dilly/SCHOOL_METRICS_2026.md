# Specialized School Metrics 2026

## Overview
These metrics are used to adjust the Dilly Campus and Pro brains for specific school environments. 

## Metric Definitions

| Metric | Target | Baseline | Adjustment |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Commit Velocity** | Pro | 3/week | +10% Weight |
| **Cross-Org Influence** | Pro | 1 repo | +15% Weight |
| **Research Density** | Campus | 1 sem | +20% Weight |
| **Hardware/Software Hybrid** | Campus | Mixed | +5% Weight |

## School Overrides

### University of Tampa (UT)
- **Entrepreneurship Bias:** +12% Grit bonus for registered LLCs.
- **Peer Scoring Alpha:** 1.15 multiplier for Peer-Verified commits.

### Georgia Tech (GT)
- **Technical Depth:** +15% multiplier for architecture-level projects.
- **Velocity Floor:** 5 commits/week minimum for "High Performance" status.

### Stanford (SU)
- **Founding Signal:** +25% bonus for YC/VC-backed student projects.
- **Velocity Index:** 0.8 base multiplier (quality over quantity).

## Graduate & Professional School Filters (Vantage Alpha)

### Medical School (MD/DO)
- **Clinical/Service Grit:** +25% weight for consistent (1yr+) clinical volunteering, shadowing, or direct patient care (EMT, Scribe, MA).
- **Academic Rigor Floor:** 3.8+ GPA baseline for "Elite" status; science GPA (BCPM) weighted at 1.5x general GPA.
- **Research Longevity:** +20% bonus for longitudinal research (2+ years in same lab) with evidence of abstract/poster/publication.
- **Service Orientation:** +15% weight for non-clinical community service showing long-term commitment (altruism signal).

### Law School (JD)
- **GPA/LSAT Dominance:** GPA weighted at 45% of smart score; LSAT presence/score provides a +20% raw smart bonus.
- **Impact Scaling:** +20% weight for "Outcome-Based" leadership (e.g., "Increased membership by X%" or "Drafted policy Y").
- **Analysis/Advocacy Density:** +15% multiplier for roles involving complex documentation, moot court, mock trial, or legal internships.
- **Professionalism Signal:** High emphasis on year-over-year progression and tenure in professional settings (avoidance of "gap" or "fluff" roles).

### General Specialized Graduate Programs
- **SOP Correlation:** Alignment between side-hustle velocity and stated research/career focus (+10% Verification score).
- **Prerequisite Mastery:** Automated cross-check of core coursework against program requirements.
