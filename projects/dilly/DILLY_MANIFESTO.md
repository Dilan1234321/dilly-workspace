# Dilly: The High-Velocity Talent Infrastructure

## 1. The Realist Mission
The job market is saturated with "optimized" resumes and AI-generated noise. Dilly bypasses the fluff by auditing **behavioral grit**, **resume-code alignment**, and **technical veracity**. We aren't a job board; we are the **Credit Score for Talent**.

## 1.1 The Dilly Truth Standard (MTS)
**Dilly is built on Truth.** 
- **Zero Hallucination:** Our optimization engine strictly refuses to invent skills, roles, or metrics.
- **Quantifiable Impact:** We transform existing user data into high-velocity action-oriented language without inflating the facts.
- **Technical Integrity:** We cross-reference claims against actual evidence. If a user hasn't proven it, we don't suggest it.

## 1.2 Diversity & Global Grit
Grit isn't just about code; it's about the obstacles overcome.
- **The International Multiplier:** We recognize that international students navigating global education and complex visa pathways exhibit a unique form of "Global Grit."
- **Holistic Auditing:** Our model accounts for cultural adaptability, multilingual proficiency, and international project scale as core talent indicators.

## 2. The Implementation (No-BS Version)
- **The Core:** The "Dilly Auditor." 
  - **Phase A (The Paper):** Resume Parsing. Extracts claims, projects, and stack.
  - **Phase B (The Proof):** GitHub/Public Activity Audit. Cross-references resume claims against actual commit history and complexity.
- **The Output:** A "Dilly Score" that becomes the industry standard for entry-level reliability.

## 3. Revenue Strategy
- **Phase 1 (The Hook):** Free audits for students. We build the most dense database of high-potential talent in the Southeast.
- **Phase 2 (The Monetization):** Sell high-fidelity access to recruiters who are tired of interviewing "frauds."
