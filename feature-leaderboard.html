Playbook

How to use ranks without spiraling

  1. Baseline after your first serious audit. Note track and percentile snapshot.
  2. Pick one dimension to move with a defined project or rewrite sprint.
  3. Ignore noise: tiny rank swings when the cohort updates. Watch four-week trends.
  4. Share wins with a mentor using concrete numbers, not vibes.

Social proof that is still honest

Students underestimate how strong average competition is. Seeing a distribution fixes delusion in both directions: sometimes you are closer than you think; sometimes you need a real Build push.
