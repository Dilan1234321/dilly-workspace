How a prep block might run

  1. Open Ask Dilly on a target role after you understand the gap bars.
  2. Run suggested prompts: motivation, conflict, technical depth, business sense.
  3. Capture bullets you want to say aloud. Paste strong lines back into the editor if they belong on the page.
  4. Schedule a coach session for mock mode when your checklist is mostly done.

Why context beats a blank chat box

General models give polished nonsense. Ask Dilly is constrained by your real scores and the role’s implied bar, so answers skew actionable instead of theatrical.

It is the bridge between browsing listings and doing disciplined prep: faster than building prompts from scratch, richer than rereading the JD for the tenth time.
