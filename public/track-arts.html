No portfolio link
Behance, Cargo, Squarespace—create a site. Add 5–8 strongest projects with context, role, and outcome. Recruiters expect a link. We tell you exactly how to format it.
Projects lack context or outcome
Every project needs: what it was, your role, who it was for, and the outcome (audience, award, client feedback). We give you copy-paste rewrites.
No exhibitions, performances, or productions
Student shows, recitals, screenings, theatre credits. Even class exhibitions or open mics count. We help you surface evidence recruiters can verify.
