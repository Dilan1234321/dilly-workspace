Cadence

Setting up your semester

  1. Import deadlines by saving roles in browse. Confirm dates on the grid.
  2. Add interviews as soon as you book. Attach prep notes or links in the event detail.
  3. Layer campus events you will actually attend. Delete fantasy entries that create noise.
  4. Review Sunday night: scan the week, adjust prep blocks, ping coach if a stack looks unfair.

Time visibility is a competitive advantage

Students who miss deadlines lose on technicality. Students who cram prep the night before lose on quality. A dedicated career calendar reduces both failure modes without pretending you have infinite hours.
