Gaps we fix

These kill humanities applications. We catch them and tell you exactly how to fix them.

No publication or writing sample Critical Submit to undergrad journals, literary magazines, or campus publications. Even a strong thesis chapter or seminar paper can serve as a writing sample.
No research evidence Critical Add thesis, conference presentation, or research assistant work. Describe methods and scope. Even "conducted archival research for honors thesis" helps.
No teaching or editorial experience Moderate Add tutoring, writing center work, or editorial for student publications. Quantify: students served, hours, pieces edited.
